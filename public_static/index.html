Our programme

Cross Border Irish Health is a programme specifically developed to assist and support Irish public healthcare.
It is designed to offer a fast and excellent hospital service in Spain to everyone entitled to public patient healthcare in Ireland.

Based on

CBIH is based on the innovative Cross-Border Healthcare Directive (CBD) that entitles people to public patient healthcare in Ireland to receive hospital treatments and services in another EU/EEA member state, being reimbursed by their home country's health service.
Read the complete directive

Our goals

Our aim is to reduce the length of time people are on waiting lists before receiving treatment in Ireland.

Our services

CBIH offers a 360-degree service to all patients applying to the programme.
From the supporting and guiding the patients in completing the entire procedure for applying to their fly and stay in Spain to be treated.

Who are we?

We are a private international organization based in Dublin and collaborating with first level hospitals in Spain, to offer a first class service to Irish patients who are willing to apply to the programme.

Our values

  • Transparency

    Our aim is to create a transparent relationship with all parties involved in the programme.

  • Commitment

    To provide a professional, caring service to assist the patient obtain the highest standard of medical care for which they are entitled to under the Cross Border Directive.

  • Create social value

    Helping the community in which you live in should be an integral part of all businesses. CBIH was created from this necessity and all operations are conducted respecting this essential value.

Medical centres

With the approval of the HSE, the programme collaborates with first level hospitals in Spain, to offer a comparable, if not better, service to Irish patients.
