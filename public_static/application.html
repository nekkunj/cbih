How the program works
step by step

1.

Fill in the Application Form on line or call a member of our team and they will assist you personally.

2.

Prepare all relevant forms required by the HSE*.

* Valid applications will be processed within 15 to 20 working days and a decision will be issued via letter. Depending on the efficiency of the HSE, the waiting period may be shorter or longer.

4.

Provide the HSE with all relevant invoices of the tretment received and you will get reimbursed**.

Online Application
How it works?

Application Form

Fill in the Application Form and forward it.
You will receive an e-mail that confirms we received your request.
The application will be processed by our admission department.
You will receive an email confirming that your application has been accepted or asking for further details.

Simply by filling up the application form does not imply any type of obbligation on your behalf.

Prepare all documents for the formal application to the HSE.

Our team will help you with documents that have to be filled in and submitted to the HSE for approval *.
We will help you personally by phone or email to correctly fill in the forms and send them to the HSE.
You will be assisted by a specialized team to ensure that everything is organised in a professional manner and that the treatment abroad is approved by the HSE.
When you have been approved by the HSE you are ready to start your treatment in Barcelona.

* Valid applications will be processed by the HSE within 15 to 20 working days and a decision will be issued via letter.
Depending on the efficiency of the HSE, the waiting period may be shorter or longer.

Your treatment in Spain

When you have been approved by the HSE you are ready to start your treatment in Spain.

After applying to the HSE and your application has been processed the decision on same will be issued by letter approving the health care service abroad.
You don’t have to worry about anything, as soon as we receive this communication, we will organize your trip to Barcelona: flights, accommodation, medical treatment and anything related to your journey.

See our services

Get reimbursed.

Provide the HSE with documents of the treatment received and get reimbursed.

Once you get home, we will send to the HSE all documents related to your journey.
Reimbursement from the HSE will be provided within 20 to 23 working days **.

Do you need to finance the treatment?
We can help you to be financed by local institutions, in order to be able to pay the treatment in advance. All money will be reimbursed by the HSE once the journey is completed.

** Reimbursement will be processed within 20 to 23 working days.
Depending on the efficiency of the HSE, the waiting period may be shorter or longer.
